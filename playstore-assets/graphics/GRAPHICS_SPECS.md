# Graphics Specifications for Google Play Store

## Required Graphics

### 1. App Icon (High-res)
- **Dimensions:** 512 x 512 pixels
- **Format:** 32-bit PNG
- **Transparency:** NOT allowed (no alpha channel)
- **File size:** Max 1 MB

#### Design Guidelines:
- Use the GlobalyOS logo/branding
- Ensure icon is recognizable at small sizes
- Avoid text (except single letters or numbers)
- Use simple, bold shapes
- Consider how it looks on different backgrounds

---

### 2. Feature Graphic
- **Dimensions:** 1024 x 500 pixels
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Transparency:** NOT allowed

#### Design Guidelines:
- This appears at the top of your store listing
- Include app name and tagline
- Show key features or UI preview
- Use brand colors

#### Suggested Content:
- Tagline: "Your Team's Complete Operating System"
- Show 1-2 key screens (dashboard, chat, or attendance)
- GlobalyOS logo

---

## Screenshots

### Phone Screenshots
- **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1920 pixels (9:16 ratio)
- **Format:** PNG or JPG (24-bit, no alpha)
- **Quantity:** Minimum 2, Maximum 8
- **Required:** Yes

### 7-inch Tablet Screenshots
- **Dimensions:** 1200 x 1920 pixels
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Quantity:** Up to 8
- **Required:** No (but recommended)

### 10-inch Tablet Screenshots
- **Dimensions:** 1600 x 2560 pixels
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **Quantity:** Up to 8
- **Required:** No (but recommended)

---

### Recommended Tools:
- **Figma** - Free, web-based, great for collaboration
- **Canva** - Easy templates for app store graphics
- **Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator** - Professional tools
- **Sketch** - Mac-only design tool

## Brand Guidelines

### GlobalyOS Colors (Use these in graphics):
- Primary: Your brand primary color
- Background: Clean white or light gray
- Accent: Your brand accent color

### Typography:
- Use clean, modern sans-serif fonts
- Ensure text is readable at small sizes
- Consider localization (leave room for longer translations)
